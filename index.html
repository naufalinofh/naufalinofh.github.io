About Fadel

The best of people are those who are most beneficial to people.

My aspiration is to bring benefits to as much people as possible. Innovation and technology are the path I believe will bring me towards the goal.

My full time focus is being Product Manager in Bibit.id where we are developing an investment platform that is easy to use for anyone who want to start investing. I believe anyone deserve better future and investing is one way to achieve the future we wanted, financially.

Being curious, I like to learn and get to know about many things including business strategy, digital technology, any kind of engineering (electronics, software, mechanical to name a few), management, physics, etc.
To sum up I am a : Learner. Aspiring physicist. Entrepreneur. Mentor. Engineer. Product guy. Hustler. Husband. Human. In that order.
